About me

I'am 21 years old, born in 2002 April. I'am a trained pattern ready to code and discover the world of development.

All idea's start simple they say, They DO! I figured, and thats where I found my passion to discover the "magic" behind computers.

It started with 3d-designs(blender) and that led to game development(c#).I gave up thinking it wasn't for me.

Three years later... I discovered hacking and I called myself a hacker until I realised I just used other peoples tools(script kiddie).

After hacking, my tower computer broke and then I found a android terminal app called termux. I refused to be a script kiddie, thats how I found python the programming language.

One year later... I finally got a laptop but got bored of windows, this is where I found and learn't about other operating systems like Linux and its distributions.

Down the line I got critizied/corrected heavily for poor code, lack of Networking knowledge and for many philosophies I wish could be true. Did I quit?.. NO!

I kept telling myself if simple idea's started the creation of complicated things then I will get there by starting simple, web development and web hacking.

I came back mentally stronger and more knowledgable. I finially used the term Penetration-Tester instead of hacker. I was brought into reality.

At the age of 20 I finially achieved a mindset for this path whereby I could confidently recreate a visual thought process of how an application works in the backend.

I'm at the point where I would not mind being a Software Developer, Penetration-Tester or a Game Developer.

I realise I have achieved the same thing in all categories which is knowing the science of a computers memory and it creates a "magical" device.

And now I'm here. Possibly on a continuous journey of non-stop seeking.
